Welcome to the Conchordance project!

Conchordance is a utility for exploring chord permutations. It combines a powerful music-theory engine with a slick user interface, to present the best tool around for exploring chords.

We're constantly improving Conchordance to bring you the most robust tool for exploring your instrument as possible.

Questions or comments? Email us your feedback!

Conchordance is Open Source

That means the code is freely available, and our team is always looking for contributors! You can find the project hosted on GitHub.
